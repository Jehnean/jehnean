Discovery

1000s of elite athletes and coaches use Driveline Baseball’s pitching training programs. Their mission is to help pitchers develop healthy, high velocity skills through their unique, metrics driven training programs. This means they need to help athletes of many different ages, skillsets, and locations, as well as coaches looking to train using their methods.

Challenges

Just a blog update…

Initially, Driveline Baseball posted that they were just looking for help “updating their blog.” Digging in, it turned out to five very different sites linking together for one company.

Which program?

With training online or in person, for high school or college athletes, youth baseball and parents, and coaches, customers were confused about what options were available and best for them.

High bounce rates

The bounce rates on the sister sites were very high, 70%+ compared to less than 10% for the main site. Google Analytic metrics indicated the main source of traffic was the blog for free “how to” articles.
